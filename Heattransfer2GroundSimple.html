Heattransfer2Ground_Simple

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Heat_Transfer

Purpose:
Calculate the heat transfer of a house to the ground with a very simple model.

Description:
Calculates the heat transfer of a house to the ground in a very simplified approach. The model doesn't correspond to standard calculations according to ISO. Use Heattransfer2GroundISO if you need a standard model.

Input:

Troom : room temperature in °C

Output:

Qdot : Thermal power to ground in W (negative when leaving the house, positive when entering the house)

Parameters and Dialog Box:

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

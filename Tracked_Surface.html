Tracked_Surface
Path: CARNOT/Weather

Purpose
Model for the calculation of axis tracking of surfaces, e.g. a collector.

Description
This block performs axis tracking of surfaces, e.g. a collector. Different modes of axis tracking can be specified by the parameters.
The tracking model uses the solar postion angles given by the Weather Data Bus WDB.
 For the definition of the collector angles see Fixed_Surface block.

Inputs:

  • WDB - Weather Data Bus

Outputs:

  • Collector position bus with  
  • inclination angle (0 = horizontal, 90 = vertical) [degree]
  • azimuth angle (0° south, -90° east) [degree]
  • rotation angle (east direction negative)[degree]

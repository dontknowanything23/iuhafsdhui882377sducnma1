function call:

y = cs_energy_cogen([timeclass], [file], [filedetail])

Inputs

timeclass - optional input parameter for the evaluation period
 1 : year (default)
 2 : month
 3 : week
 4 : day
 5 : hour
file - optional character string with the name of the .mat file
 created by the data logger. If not given during function call, the
 function itsself will open an input window for the choice of the file.
 Files written by the Logger_Cogen have the format 'cogenoutput*.mat'
filedetail - optional character string with the name of the .mat file
 created by the enabled data logger in the Logger_Cogen block.
 This file contain the same information as file but they are stored only
 when the motor is working.
 Files written by the Logger_Cogen have the format 'cogendetailoutput*.mat'
file and, if given filedetail, must contain the following information in timeseries:
 cogen.Th_in : inlet temperature in degree Celsius
 cogen.Th_out : outlet temperature in degree Celsius
 cogen.MassH : integrated massflow in kg
 cogen.Qh :   thermal energy to flowline in Joule
 cogen.Tmean : average cogen unit temperature in degree Celsius
 cogen.MassFuel : mass of consumed fuel in kg
 cogen.EnergyFuel : energy from fuel in J
 cogen.Welec_in : consumed electric energy in J
 cogen.CostFuel : fuel costs in -
 cogen.Mgas_H2O : H2O emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_CO2 : CO2 emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_O2 :  O2 emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_SO2 :  SO2 emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_N2 :  N2 emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_NOx : NOx emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_CO : CO emissions in kg
 cogen.Mgas_CxHy : CxHy emissions in kg
 cogen.efficiency : efficiency
 cogen.Cycles : number of on/off cycles
 cogen.OpTime : total working time of the cogeneration
 cogen.Efficiency_Thermal : thermal efficiency
 cogen.Efficiency_Electric : electric efficiency
 cogen.Welec_out : electric energy produced in J
 cogen.CostElecBuy : costs of consumed electric energy in -
 cogen.CostElecSell : revenues of sold electric energy in -

Outputs

Matrix where for timeclass 1 the first row has the annual values.
For timeclass 2 the 12 rows contain the monthly values.
Columns:
  1   time of simulation or timeclass start
  2   time of simulation or timeclass end
  3   heating energy in Joule
  4   produced electric energy in J
  5   consumed electric energy in Joule
  6   consumed fuel energy in Joule
  7   average total efficiency
  8   average thermal efficiency
  9   average electric efficiency
 10   number of on/off cycles
 11   operation time in seconds
 12   average massflow in kg/s
 13   consumed fuel mass in kg
 14   CO2 emissions in kg
 15   mean cogen unit inlet temperature degree Celsius *)
 16   mean cogen unit outlet temperature degree Celsius *)
 17   mean cogen unit temperature degree Celsius *)
 18   costs of fuel in -
 19   costs of consumed electric energy in -
 20   revenues for produced electric energy in -
*) The values are only calculated when 'filedetail' is given.

Description

Evaluation of data written by Logger_Cogen. The result
of the function is the energy in annual, monthly, ... values, depending
on the timeclass. For timeclass 1 the first row has the annual values.
For timeclass 2 the 12 rows contain the monthly values.
The outputs are organized in groups: time, energy, efficiency,
operation, average massflows, average temperatures, economics

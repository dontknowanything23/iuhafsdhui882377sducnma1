Hot_Water_T_Mdot_Profile

Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping





Purpose:
Hot water consumption with a setpoint temperature and massflow profile.

Description:
There are different methods to evaluate the hot water comfort (see QAIST 2012). In this model the hot water draw-off is defined by a setpoint temperature and a massflow. The tapping starts at the specified time with the specified flow-rate. The draw-off ends when the specified duration is reached. The model includes a mixing valve trying to maintain the setpoint temperature (defined as hot water temperature). The necessary massflow through the cylinder is calculated by this block and set in the outport to the cylinder.
The model uses the mdot_rep_seq block to create the massflow profile. The T_cold_Water_Sine block is used to create a seasonally varying cold water temperature.

Input:

THBcylinder : hot water Thermo-Hydraulic Bus from the DHW cylinder (or fresh water station)

Output:

THB_cylinder : cold water Thermo-Hydraulic Bus to the DHW cylinder (or fresh water station)
THB_to_tap : warm water Thermo-Hydraulic Bus to the tap
Tset : temperature set point of the warm water in °C

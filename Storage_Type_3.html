Storage_Type_3

Path: CARNOT/Storage/Thermal







Purpose:
Store with two immersed heat exchangers, two discharging pipe connections and an electric heater.

Description:
Store with two immersed heat exchangers according to the EN12977 model.
Typical application: Domestic hot water storage with dhw circulation, a heat exchanger for solar thermal collectors and a backup heater.
The model uses the multiport storage model, see Storage_TypeN for details.

Input:

Tamb : ambient temperature in °C (for thermal losses)
THB_HX_up : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus inlet of the upper heat exchanger
THB_HX_low : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus inlet of the lower heat exchanger
THB_dhw : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the cold water inlet
THB_circ : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of circulation return
Pel : power of the electric heater in W

Output:

Sdat : Storage data bus
Tsensors : value of temperature sensors in °C
THB_hx_up : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus outlet of the upper heat exchanger
THB_hx_low : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus outlet of the lower heat exchanger
THBdhw : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the hot water oulet
THBcirc : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of circulation outlet

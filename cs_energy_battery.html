Function Call

y = cs_energy_battery([timeclass], [file], [filedetail])

Inputs

timeclass - optional input parameter for the evaluation period
 1 : year (default)
 2 : month
 3 : week
 4 : day
 5 : hour
file - optional character string with the name of the .mat file
 created by the data logger. If not given during function call, the
 function itsself will open an input window for the choice of the file.
 Files written by the Logger_Battery have the format 'batteryoutput*.mat'
filedetail - optional character string with the name of the .mat file
 created by the enabled data logger in the Logger_Battery block.
 This file contain the same information as file but they are stored only
 when the battery is charged or discharged.
 Files written by the Logger_Battery have the format 'batterydetailoutput*.mat'
The file contains the following information in timeseries :
 batt.EnergySetpointCharge   AC energy charged at setpoint in J
 batt.EnergySetpointDisarge  AC energy discharged at setpoint in J
 batt.EnergyChargeAC         energy charged at AC connection in J
 batt.EnergyDischargeAC      energy discharged at AC connection in J
 batt.EnergyChargeDC         energy charged on DC side (into the cell) in J
 batt.EnergyDischargeDC      energy discharged on DC side (from the cell) in J
 batt.Energy_Excess          energy excess (which could not be stored, typically sent to grid) in J
 batt.Energy_Deficit         energy deficit (which could not be deliverey by the battery, typically taken from grid) in J
 batt.Energy                 energy in the battery in J
 batt.SOC                    state of charge
 batt.Capacity               capacity of the batter in J
 batt.Time_Charging          duration of charging in s
 batt.Time_Discharging       duration of discharging in s
 batt.Cycles_Charging        number of charging cycles
 batt.Cycles_Discharging     number of discharging cycles

Outputs

Matrix where for timeclass 1 the first row has the annual values.
For timeclass 2 the 12 rows contain the monthly values.
Columns:
  1   time of simulation or timeclass start
  2   time of simulation or timeclass end
  3   energy charged at AC connection in J
  4   energy discharged at AC connection in Joule
  5   energy charged into the cell in J
  6   energy discharged from the cell in J
  7   desired energy charged at AC connection in J
  8   desired energy discharged at AC connection in J
  9   energy difference between beginning and end of the timeclass in J
 10   state of charge difference between beginning and end of the timeclass
 11   energy losses in the battery in J
 12   number of charging cycles
 13   number of discharging cycles
 14   number of energy cycles (defined for the DC side)
 15   duration of charging in s
 16   duration of discharging in s

Description

Evaluation of data written by Logger_Battery. The result
of the function is the energy in annual, monthly, ... values, depending
on the timeclass. For timeclass 1 the first row has the annual values.
For timeclass 2 the 12 rows contain the monthly values.
The outputs are organized in groups: time, energy, efficiency,
operation, average massflows, average temperatures, economics

VerticalAirTemperatureDifference

Path: CARNOT/Outputs/Comfort

Purpose:
This block calculates the percentage of dissatisfied (PD) due to vertical air temperature differences according to ISO 7730.

Description:
From ISO 7730 : A high vertical air temperature difference in the area between the head and the feet can lead to discomfort. The percentage of dissatisfied (PD) due to vertical air tempeature is calculated by the following equation. Input:

DeltaT : vertical temperature difference between ankles and head in K

Output:

PD : percentage of dissatisfied in %

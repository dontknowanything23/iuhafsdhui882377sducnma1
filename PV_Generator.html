PV_Generator

Path: CARNOT/Source/Electric_Generator




Purpose:
Model of a photovoltaic generator (= array of several PV modules).

Description:
This block simultes a PV genertor and calculates the output of the module in W based on the module characteristic parameters:

  • Pmax: Peak Power (Wp) at Standard Test Conditions (STC) in W
  • delta_Wp_K: Temperature coefficient for power drop from of Pmax in 1/K

The power of the generator is calculated by:

     P = Solar_Radiation/(1000 W/m2) * Incidence_Angle_Modifier * Pmax * delta_Wp_K * (25 °C - Tamb - (40 K)*Solar_Radiation/(1000 W/m2))

The temperature model of the module has the following assumptions:

  • 25 °C module temperature at standard test conditions (STC)
  • 40 K temperature difference to ambient at solar radiation of 1000 W/m2

The Incidence_Angle_Modifier is 1 for vertical direct solar radiation. It follows the reflection law of Fresnel.

Input:

WDB : Weather Data Bus
position : vector with the orientation of the PV modules, see Fixed_Surface for the definition of the angles

Output:

EB_power : Electric bus EB with the power of the PV generator in W
the block EBCreator is used, the power is fed to line 1 only.
PVdat : data bus for the evaluation of the PV generator

The evaluation data bus contains:

P_DC direct current power W
SolarRadiation_global_surface global solar radiation on the surface of the PV modules W/m2
Tcell cell temperature of the modules °C
P_peak_PV peak power of the PV generator (power at STC) W

Thermostat_Proportional

Path: CARNOT/Control


Purpose:
Thermostatic proportional controller for a hydraulic valve.

Description:
Thermostatic controller with a proportional characteristics. The incomming room temperature is delayed by the thermal inertia (first order transfer function). The mechanic is modeled with a play.

Input:

T : actual temperature in °C
Tset : set point temperature in °C

Output:

pos : mechanical postion of the valve in [0..1]

Pressure_Drop_changeD

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Pressure_Drop


Purpose:
The block calculates the pressure drop of a diameter change.

Description:
The block calculates the pressure drop of a sudden diameter change at the inlet of a pipe or hydraulic component. Effects of diffusers or nozzles are not taken into account. The pressure drop is included in the pressure of the THB. The pressure coefficients clq are also adapted.
Calculation is done for Flow_ID > 10000, see Basic Concepts for details.

Input:

THBin : incomming Thermo Hydraulic Bus

Output:

THB : leaving Thermo Hydraulic Bus

Parameters and Dialog Box:

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

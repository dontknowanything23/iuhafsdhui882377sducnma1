createCdat

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Thermal_Models

 

 

 

Purpose:
Create the collector data bus (Cdat) for solar thermal collector models.

Description:
All relevant informations for further evaluation of the solar collector are combined in this structure.  

Input:

acol : collector surface in m² (ISO 9806 : brutto surface)
THBin : inlet Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the collector
THBout : oulet Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the collector
Qdot : thermal power of the collector in W
Tmean : average temperature of the collector in °C
WDB : Weather Data Bus

Output:

Cdat : Collector Data Bus with the informations of the input

 

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

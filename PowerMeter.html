PowerMeter

Path: CARNOT/Control

Purpose:
The block calculates the thermal power between inlet and outlet of a component.

Description:
The block shows the power [W] in one Thermo-Hydraulic Bus (THB) between the inlet and the outlet of one component. If the temperature at the outlet is above the temperature at the inlet, the calculated power is positive.

     power = heat_capacity * mass-flow * (T_out - T_in)

Connect THB_in to the THB entering the component and THB_out to the THB at the outlet.

Input:

THB_out : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus at the component outlet
THB_in : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus at the component inlet

Output:

power : thermal power in W

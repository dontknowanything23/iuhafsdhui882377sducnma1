PhaseCheck

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Material_Properties


Purpose:
The block checks, if the fluid is still in the liquid phase. Simulation is stopped if the fluid is not liquid. This block applies specially for water but also some other fluids.

Description:
The block checks according to the inputs temperature T, pressure p and fluid mixture (for 2-phase- fluids, see FluidEnum), if the fluid is still in the same phase, for example if water is not steam or ice. The vapour pressure is calculated by CARLIB function vapourpressure.

Temperature limits for different fluids:

Water :
T < 0 °C : The water in your system is ice.
system pressure p < vapor pressure : The water in your system is steam.

Air :
T < -273.15 °C : Your system temperature is below 0 K.

Cotton oil :
T < -20 °C : The oil in your system is solid.
T > 450 °C : The oil in your system has cracked.

Silicone oil:
T < -50 °C : The oil in your system is solid.
T > 450 °C : The oil in your system has cracked.

Water glycol mix:
T < ((-222.2222*fluid_mix+61.9048)*fluid_mix-48.7302)*fluid_mix) : The water-glycol mix in your system is ice.
system pressure p < vapour pressure : The water-glycol in your system is steam

Input:

THBin : incomming Thermo-Hydraulic Bus

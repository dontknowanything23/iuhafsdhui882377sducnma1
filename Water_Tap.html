Water_Tap

Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping


Purpose:
This block models a water tap.

Description:
The block is a constant pump that sets a constant mass flow rate at a constant temperature. The massflow at the outlet is set by the input "mdot".

Input:

mdot : mass flow of the water in kg/s
Tc : cold water temperature in °C

Output:

THB : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the water flow

wall_out_IR_radiation

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Heat_Transfer

Purpose:
Calculate the radiative heat transfer of a surface.

Description:
Infrared radiation heat transfer of a vertical wall to the Tsky and ground. The ground is at Tamb. The vertical wall has a view on 50% ground and 50% sky (true for vertical walls). The longwave radiation emissiviy of the ground and the sky is assumed to be 1.
The block used in the alfa_wall_out block.
Radiative heat transfer is calculated by the correlation:

     qrad = sigma * emission * ( 1/2 *(Tsky4 - Twall4) + 1/2 * (Tamb^4 - Twall^4) )

with emission : emissivity factor of the surface (0.87 for window glass)

Input:

WDB : Weather Data Bus
Ts : temperature of window surface (glass surface) to the outside in °C

Output:

[sun_t] : vector with sun data:
[incidence angle, direct and diffuse radiation at tilted window surface]
qdots1 : specific heat flow into wall construction in W/m2

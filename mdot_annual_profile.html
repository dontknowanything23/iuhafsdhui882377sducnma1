mdot_annual_profile

Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping

Purpose:
Create a mass flow depending on a user defined load profile.

Description:
In the mask of the block, you can specify how much the massflow varies during the year and during a day. It's possible to set different load profile for weekdays and weekend.

Output:

mdot : mass flow in kg/s

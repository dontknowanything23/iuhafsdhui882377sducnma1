T_cold_Water_Location

Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping


Purpose:
Create a seasonally varying cold water temperature for different locations.

Description:
The block creates a varying cold water temperature over the year for different locations. The uses a linear curve between minimum and maximum or an approximation with a sine curve. The mathematical model is the same as for the block T_cold_Water_Sine :

     Tc = Tmean + Tamplitude * sin( 2*PI*(SimulationDay + DayPhaseShift) / 365 )

The locations can be: Athens, Barcelona, Lyon, Madrid, Stockholm, Wuerzburg and Zuerich. The following parmeters have been fitted for the different locations:

Location Tmean in °C Tamplitude in °C DayPhaseShift
Athens 15.0 5.0 -137
Barcelona 14.5 4.5 -137
Lyon 12.0 4.0 -137
Madrid 14.5 4.5 -137
Stockholm 8.5 6.4 -137
Wuerzburg 10.0 3.0 -137
Zuerich 9.7 6.3 -137

Output:

Tc : cold water temperature in °C

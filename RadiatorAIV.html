Radiator_AIV

Path: CARNOT/Source/Heat_Exchanger

Purpose:
This block models the radiators of a house heating system.

Description:
The model adapts the Radiator model to the AIB and S-Buses for the detailed room model, see Room_Radiator and Room_Floor_Heating.

Input:

AIB : Air infiltration bus
THB_in : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the flow line

Output:

S : S-bus
THB_out : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the return line

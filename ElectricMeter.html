ElectricMeter
Path: CARNOT/Control

Purpose
The block integrated electric power in the EB to a positive and negative energy.

Description
The block shows the power [W], positive and negative energy [J] in the EB.
Negative energy is typically counted as energy from the grid, positive energy as energy created in the system (and eventually fed to the grid).
 The Reset input resets the energy counter to zero with a rising flank.

Inputs:

  • EB : Electric Bus  
  • Reset (rising)

Outputs:

  • positive energy in [J]
  • electric power in [W]
  • negative energy in [J]

Parameters and Dialog Box

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No

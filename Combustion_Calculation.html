Combustion_Calculation

Path: CARNOT/Basic/ThermalModels




Purpose:
Calculation of exhaust gas compostion, fuel consumption and fuel costs of a boiler.

Description:
Calculates the exhaust gas composition using the mass fractions of hydrogen, carbon, oxygen, sulfur, nitrogen and water in the fuel air mixture at the inlet of the burner. These values are defines by the Fuel block.
The fuel mass flow is calculated by the calorific value of the fuel without and with condensation.

Input:

Enable : input to activate the combustion calculation, this value is set by the Fuel block
Qdot_fuel : Fuel power in W
efficiency : efficiency = Thermal power / Fuel power, this value must be calculated by the boiler model
Pelec : electric power consumption in W
Tm : mean temperature in °C
fuel : Fuel bus creted by Fuel block

Output:

eco : Eco-bus, see 2.1.5 Eco bus in 2_Basic_Concepts

Velocity

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Hydraulics



Purpose:
Calculates the velocity in a pipe in [m/s].

Description:
Calculates the velocity in a pipe according to the inputs.
Assuming a round pipe the block uses the equation:

v = mdot/(density*cross section)

Input:

T : temperature in °C
mdot : mass flow in kg/s
p : pressure in Pa
fluid, mix : vector with 2 elements: fluid type) and fluid mixture
d : inner diameter of the pipe

Output:

v : velocity in m/s
density : density of the fluid in kg/m3

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

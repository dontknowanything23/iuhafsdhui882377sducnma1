Pressuredrop

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Pressure_Drop



Purpose:
The block calculates the pressure drop in a hydraulic component.

Description:
The block calculates the pressure drop in a component according to the Carnot pressure drop equation:

     dp = con + mdot*lin + mdot2*qua

Input:

mdot : mass flow in kg/s
p_in : pressure at the inlet in Pa
clq_in : vector with the pressure drop coefficients [con, lin, qua] at the inlet
con : constant pressure drop coefficient of the component in Pa
lin : linear pressure drop coefficient of the component in Pa/(kg/s)
qua : quadratic pressure drop coefficient of the component in Pa/(kg/s)2

Output:

p_out : pressure at the outlet in Pa
clq_in : vector with the pressure drop coefficients [con, lin, qua] at the outlet
dp : difference of pressure between inlet an outlet in Pa

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

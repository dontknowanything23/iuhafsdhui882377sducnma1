Isolated_Cover

Path: CARNOT/Storage/Thermal/surface_models

Purpose:
Isolated cover model to calculate the ground characteristic for the Ground_Storage calculation.

Description:
The model describes the absorption of the solar radiation and the heat loss by the wind. The block is used in the subsystem of the Ground_Storage.

Input:

Tamb : ambient temperature above the cover in °C

Output:

Tamb_utop_Iabs : vector [Tamb in °C, loss coefficient of the cover in W/m2/K, solar radiation on the cover in W/m2

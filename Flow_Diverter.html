Flow_Diverter

Path: CARNOT/Hydraulics



Purpose:
Divide the entering mass flow in two mass flows according to the fraction fdiv.

Description:
Divide the entering mass flow in two mass flows according to the fraction fdiv. All other properties are fed through.

     mdot(THB1) = fdiv * mdot(THB)

     mdot(THB2) = (1-fdiv) * mdot(THB)

Connect the output clq of this block to the input of the corresponding Flow_Mixer. The corresponding flow mixer is the flow mixer which combines the flow of the two branches THB1 and THB2.
The signal fdiv is calculated in the flow mixer. Feed back fdiv from the flow mixer to this block to assure a proper flow diversion according to the pressure drop in the branches 1 and 2 (see example_Hydraulic_FlowDiverter.slx). Use fdiv as a control input if you want to set the flow diversion manually or by a controller (see example_Hydraulic_FlowDiverterExternalControl.slx).

Input:

THB : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus
fdiv : Flow division rate

Output:

THB1 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of branch 1
THB2 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of branch 2
clq : bus with massflow and pressure drop coefficients in THB (information is needed by the corresponding flow mixer)

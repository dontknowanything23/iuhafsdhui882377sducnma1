function call:

y = cs_energy_boiler([timeclass], [file], [filedetail])

Inputs

timeclass - optional input parameter for the evaluation period
 1 : year (default)
 2 : month
 3 : week
 4 : day
 5 : hour
file - optional character string with the name of the .mat file
 created by the data logger. If not given during function call, the
 function itsself will open an input window for the choice of the file.
 Files written by the Logger_Storage have the format 'storageoutput*.mat'
filedetail - optional character string with the name of the .mat file
 created by the enabled data logger in the Logger_Storage block.
 This file contain the same information as file but they are stored only
 when the burner is working.
 Files written by the Logger_Storage have the format 'storagedetailoutput*.mat'
file and, if given filedetail, must contain the following information in timeseries:
 boiler.Th_in        inlet temperature (heating) in °C
 boiler.Th_out       outlet temperature (heating) in °C
 boiler.MassH        integral of the mass flow in kg
 boiler.Qh           heating energy in J
 boiler.Th_mean      average operation temperature in °C
 boiler.MassFuel     mass of consumed fuel in kg
 boiler.EnergyFuel   energy of consumed fuel in J
 boiler.Welec_in     used electric energy in J
 boiler.CostFuel     costs of fuel in -
 boiler.MassH2O      mass of water in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassCO2      mass of CO2 in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassO2       mass of (remaining) O2 in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassSO2      mass of SO2 in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassN2       mass of N2 in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassNOx      mass of NOx in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassCO       mass of CO in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.MassCxHy     mass of CxHy in the fume gas in kg
 boiler.Cycles       number of on/off cycles
 boiler.OpTime       operation time of the boiler in s
 boiler.CostElecBuy  costs of the electric energy in -

Outputs

y - Matrix with the columns
  1   start of the simulation or timeclass in s
  2   end of the simulation or timeclass in s
  3   heating energy in Joule (positive values for T_out > T_in)
  4   consumed electric energy in Joule
  5   consumed fuel energy in Joule
  6   average efficiency
  7   number of on/off cycles
  8   operation time in seconds
  9   average boiler massflow in kg/s
 10   consumed fuel mass in kg
 11   CO2 emissions in kg
 12   average boiler inlet temperature degree Celsius *)
 13   average boiler outlet temperature degree Celsius *)
 14   average boiler temperature degree Celsius *)
 15   costs the consumed electric energy
 16   costs for the fuel
for timeclass 1 the first row has the annual values
for timeclass 2 the 12 rows contain the monthly values
*) The values are only calculated when 'filedetail' is given.

Description

Evaluation of data written by Logger_Boiler. The result
of the function is the energy in annual, monthly, ... values, depending
on the timeclass. For timeclass 1 the first row has the annual values.
For timeclass 2 the 12 rows contain the monthly values.
The outputs are organized in groups: time, energy, efficiency,
operation, average massflows, average temperatures, economics

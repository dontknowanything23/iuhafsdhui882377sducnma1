Table of fluids

ID  Fluid        Description
1   WATER        water or steam
                 fluid_mix is the vapourcontent in the 2-phase region
                 liquid water: temperature range  0°C to 374.15°C
                 pressure range: pressure dependance is not considered
                 steam: temperature range 0°C to 800 °C
                 pressure range: 600 Pa to 45 MPa
2   AIR          fluid_mix is (kg water) / (kg air)
                 temperature range: -150°C to 1000°C
                 pressure range: ideal gas assumption
3   COTOIL       cotton oil
                 temperature range:    0°C to 320°C
                 pressure dependance is not considered
4   SILOIL       silicone oil
                 temperature range: - 50°C to 400°C
                 pressure dependance is not considered
5   WATERGLYCOL  water glycol mixture (Adunka, 1991)
                 fluid_mix gives the volume-fraction of glycol
                 temperature range: - 20°C to 200°C
                 pressure dependance is not considered
6   TYFOCOR_LS   water-propylenglycol mixture with 42% glycol (Tyforop 2000)
                 temperature range : -30°C to 120°C
                 pressure dependance is not considered
7   WATER_CONSTANT liquid water (EN 12975, 2006)
                 temperature : 20°C
                 pressure : 1013 hPa
8   AIR_CONSTANT dry air (VDI 1988)
                 temperature : 20°C
                 pressure : 1013 hPa
9   ETHYLENGLYCOL_WATER
                 mixture of ethylen-glycol and water (Conde 2011)
                 fluid_mix gives the volume-fraction of ethylen glycol
                 temperature range: - 20°C to 200°C
                 pressure dependance is not considered
10  PROPYPENGLYCOL_WATER
                 mixture of propylen-glycol and water (Conde 2011)
                 fluid_mix gives the volume-fraction of propylen glycol
                 temperature range: - 20°C to 200°C
                 pressure dependance is not considered

Description

FluidEnum is a Simunlink Class of IntEnumType.
available methods are:
FluidNumber(FluidEnum.X) : enumeration number of the fluid (double)
see also carlib

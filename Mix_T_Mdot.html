Mix_T_Mdot

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Hydraulics

Purpose:
Calculation of mixed values of temperature and mass flow after a Tee-piece.

Description:
The block calculates the values of temperature and mass flow rate after the reassembly of two flows streams in a Tee-piece. The mass flows are added. According to the selected calculation method the temperature is weighted according to the mass flows:

     Tmix = (T1*mdot1 + T2*mdot2)/(mdot1+mdot2)

or determined by the enthalpy balance:

     h = (h1*mdot1 + h2*mdot2)/(mdot1+mdot2)

The enthalpy (h1, h2) is calculated by the function "enthalpy" in the CARLIB and transformed to a temperature by an iteration method in the function "enthalpy2temperature".

Input:

T1 : temperature of the flow 1 in °C
mdot1 : mass flow of the flow 1 in kg/s
T2 : temperature of the flow 2 in °C
mdot2 : mass flow of the flow 2 in kg/s

Output:

T : temperature of the mixed flow in °C
mdot : mass flow of the mixed flow in kg/s

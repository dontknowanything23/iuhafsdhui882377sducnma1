OnOff_switch

Path: CARNOT/Control

Purpose:
Manual switch to create a control signal of 0 or 1.

Description:
Double-click on the block to change the output signal (0 to 1 or 1 to 0).

Output:

Out1 : control signal (0 or 1)

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : No
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

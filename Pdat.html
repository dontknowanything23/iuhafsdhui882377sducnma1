Pdat

Path: CARNOT/Hydraulics

Purpose:
Provide operation informations of the pump or fan models in a bus.

Description:
The data points in the Pdat bus are:

THB : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the outgoing fluid flow
PowerElectric : electric power consumption in W
PressureHead : pressure difference between inlet and outlet in Pa
ControlActual : currently used value of the control signal
ControlSetpoint : input control signal

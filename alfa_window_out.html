alfa_window_out

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Heat_Transfer


Purpose:
Calculate the heat transfer of a window to the outdoor.

Description:
Convective and radiative heat transfer at the surface of a VERTICAL window towards outdoor ambient.
The block is disigned to be used in the Window block. It is also used in Wall_with_Window block.
The model uses the Weather Data Bus as input for the outdoor ambient conditions.
Convective heat transfer is calculated by the correlation:

     U = 2.8 + 3 * v_wind

with v_wind : wind velocity in m/s

Radiative heat transfer is calculated by the wall_out_IR_radiation block.

Input:

WDB : Weather Data Bus
Ts : temperature of window surface (glass surface) to the outside in °C

Output:

[sun_t] : vector with sun data:
[incidence angle, direct and diffuse radiation at tilted window surface]
qdots1 : specific heat flow into wall construction in W/m2

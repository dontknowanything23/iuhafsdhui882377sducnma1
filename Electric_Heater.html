Electric_Heater

Path: CARNOT/Source/Boiler


Purpose:
This block calculates the heat input from electrical heating.

Description:
The block models an electrical heating. It heats the entering flow which is defined by the Thermo-Hydraulic Bus with electrical power entering the block. The calculation is performed using the equation for heat input to a fluid:

     T(outlet) = T(inlet) + Heating_Power/(mdot*cp)

with:

T : temperature in °C
Heating_Power : electric power in W
mdot : mass flow in kg/s
cp : heat capacity in J/kg/K

To avoid a division by zero the massflow is limited to values above 1e-5 kg/s. A PT1 transfer function with a time-constant of 60 s is used the model the thermal inertial of the electric heater.
The Heating_Power is not limited to values >= 0. You may model a thermal load with an negative Heating_Power.

Input:

THBin : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the incomming flow
Heating_Power : power input to the flow in W

Output:

THBout : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the leaving flow

RealTime

Path: CARNOT/Toolbox

Purpose:
This block gives the system time in seconds.

Description:
The underlying s-function of the block takes the current time of your computer (function localtime in time.h). The year is not considered which means an output of 0 s on January, 1st, 00:00:00. Leap years are taken into account by a simplified method (leap year is when the mod(year,4) == 0).

Output:

time : local time in s (0 s = January, 1st, 00:00:00)

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : No
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

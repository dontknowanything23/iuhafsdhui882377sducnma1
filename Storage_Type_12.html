Storage_Type_12

Path: CARNOT/Storage/Thermal






Purpose:
Buffer store with two charging and two discharging pipes and an electric heater.

Description:
The buffer is used for decoupling thermal energy production and load. Typical application: Buffer store in a heating or cooling system with two sources (e.g. a heat pump and a backup boiler) and two loads (e.g. two heating circuits).
The model uses the multiport storage model, see Storage_TypeN for details.

Input:

Tamb : ambient temperature in °C (for thermal losses)
THB_chg1 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the incomming first charging port (flow line charge)
THB_chg2 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the incomming second charging port (flow line charge)
THB_dis1 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the incomming first discharging port (return discharge)
THB_dis2 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the incomming second discharging port (return discharge)
Pel : power of the electric heater in W

Output:

Sdat : Storage data bus
Tsensors : value of temperature sensors in °C
THBchg1 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the outgoing first charging port (return charge)
THBchg : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the outgoing second charging port (return charge)
THBdis1 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the outgoing first discharging port (flow line discharge)
THBdis2 : Thermo-Hydraulic Bus of the outgoing second discharging port (flow line discharge)

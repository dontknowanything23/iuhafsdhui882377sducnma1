Radiation_Divison

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Weather_and_Sun

Purpose:
Calculates the direct and diffusive radiation on the horizontal surface as parts of the global radiation and the sun position.

Description:
The direct and diffusive radiation are calculated by the formulas of Duffie/Beckman, 2020. The model calculates the sun_position described by the three sun-angles zenith-angle, azimuth-angle and the angle between collector normal and the sun. The calculation is carried out based the Orgill and Hollands model (1977).
This block is also available as workspace function
radiationdivision

Input:

Time : time of the simulation in s (January 1st, 00:00:00 = 0 s)
Iglb : global solar radiation on a horizontal surface in W/m2

Output:

Idir : direct solar radiation on a horizontal surface in W/m2
Idfu : diffuse solar radiation on a horizontal surface in W/m2
SunAngle : angle between the sun and the horizon in °
Zenith : angle between the sun and the zenith in °
Azimuth : angle between the sun and the south (east = -90°, west = +90°) in °

Parameters and Dialog Box:

Literature:
Duffie, J., Beckman, W., Blair, N.: Solar Engineering of thermal processes, John Wiley & Sons, Inc. 2020
J.F. Orgill, K.G.T. Hollands: Correlation equation for hourly diffuse radiation on a horizontal surface, Solar Energy, Vol, 19(4), 1977

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No

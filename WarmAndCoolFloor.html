WarmAndCoolFloor

Path: CARNOT/Outputs/Comfort

Purpose:
This block calculates the percentage of dissatisfied with the floor temperature according to ISO 7730.

Description:
From ISO 7730 : If the floor is too warm or too cold, people in the room may feel uncomfortable due to the heat sensation of their feet. For people wearing lightweight shoes suitable for indoor use, it is not the suitable for indoor use, it is not the material of the floor covering but the floor temperature that is decisive for the comfort is decisive.

FloorTemperature : temperature of the floor in °C
